Job Description
The State is pursuing an HR Digital Transformation initiative to transition from multiple disparate systems to a digitally intuitive, user friendly, integrated solution. The objective of the HR Digital Transformation project is to procure and implement a single source solution that will enhance the capabilities provided in the current solutions. These include Cornerstone on Demand for Learning Management and Performance Management, PeopleAdmin for Position Description Management, NeoGov for Recruitment/Applicant Tracking, SAP as the on-premise HR ERP solution and includes Organization Management, Personnel Administration, workforce time management, Benefits, and Reporting). The candidate will work under the supervision of the OSHR CIO and will assist in completing the duties and deliverables described below. The candidate will be responsible for reviewing and providing feedback to the CIO and the HCM Program Director, as necessary, on short and long-term strategies, evaluation and analysis of various software solutions, vendor proposals and project deliverables (business case, status reports, requirements documents, stakeholder communications, etc.) The selected candidate must possess proven ERP HR experience in state government with 2 or more full life cycle implementations of large, complex statewide solutions. North Carolina government experience is required. The candidate must be able to ascertain the State of North Carolina's needs based on capabilities, then assist in developing detailed strategies and required steps, as well as project timelines necessary to achieve the desired outcomes. The candidate will review and provide feedback on large, complex reports for OSHR executive management and state legislative offices/personnel, adhering to strict timelines for completion.
